How to Play Car Crasher

Controls:

- Use arrow keys or change to control your car, going left or right between lanes.

- Press Tab to pause the game. You can after resume the game or go back to the main menu.

- Press ESC to exit the game. Navigate the main menu with Up and Down. Press Enter to choose your game option. You can use the menu to change car, key binds or the difficulty of the game.

Gameplay:

- Avoid obstacles on the road, or else you will lose one of your three lives!

- Collect hearts to gain extra lives

- Try to survive as long as possible, and break the score record!
