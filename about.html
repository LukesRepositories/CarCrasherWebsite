About Car Crasher

Car Crasher is a high-stakes police chase game where players must skilfully navigate a four-lane motorway while evading law enforcement. The game's core objective is to survive as long as possible by avoiding various obstacles and collecting power-ups along the way.

Players start with three lives and must carefully weave through traffic, avoiding collisions with trash cans, road signs, civilian cars, and trucks. Each collision costs a life, but players can replenish their health by collecting heart power-ups scattered throughout the course.

Taking inspiration from Subway Surfers' engaging endless runner gameplay mechanics, the game combines this with a classic top-down perspective reminiscent of Grand Theft Auto 2's visual style, creating a unique and nostalgic gaming experience.

Features:

Through an intuitive main menu interface, players can:

  • Jump straight into the action
  • Select their getaway vehicle
  • Adjust difficulty settings
  • Customize their control scheme with different key bindings
